KNOW ME

Morning! I'm PALAK, an aspiring communication designer / writer / photographer / researcher / scribbler / freedom and hard work enjoyer. Based in India, drawn to the power of art and design as a tool for exploration and storytelling.

Here's small poem to best describe myself-

I carry a theory without any evidence for it,

only an ever-changing belief in the craft

and its power to change me,

to shape me into someone

more observant, more open, and skilled.


All is wonder to eyes as fresh as mine,

But the real magic or tragedy lies in the daily work,

in sustaining an honest dialogue with it.


It asks only that I stay,

working with patience and grit

allowing the process to shape me

As much as I shape it.
